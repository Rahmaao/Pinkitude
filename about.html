About Pinkitude

The Pinkitude Place was conceptualized in the year 2015 and became registered in 2016. Over the years, up until now, we have been evolving our approach to community development to meet the dynamic needs of the present community.
For the larger part of our operations we focus on Girl Child Advocacy, Women Empowerment and Youth Development. Our presense is registered in Lagos state and Adamawa state and so far we have been able to empower and impact over five thousand (5,000) Nigerian Youth in both states

Mission

Our mission is to equip and empower young people with the necessary knowledge, skills and abilities into becoming agents of light within their immediate community.

Vision

It is our vision to be a shinning light.
