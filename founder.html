Emmanuel Oluwaseyi Odunusi

Founder, Pinkitude Place

Odunusi Emmanuel Oluwaseyi is an enthusiast when it comes to working with young people. His desire to mould and develop young minds into formidable people led him to establishing Pinkitude Place in 2016. The Pinkitude Place aims to equip and empower young people with necessary skills to become lights in the society. He has worked and trained over 5,000 young people on personal development through his modules on emotional intelligence, temperaments, identity crisis and hierarchy of needs and the likes. Furthermore, he is a humanitarian and community development specialist who has worked with NGOs, INGOs and various top-notched schools.

Sunshine, as he is fondly called, graduated with a B.sc in Economics in 2012 from Ajayi Crowther University. In 2017, he bagged the prestigious MBA degree from Obafemi Awolowo University (OAU). Currently, he is a graduate student of The American University of Nigeria where he is pursuing a masters degree in Business Administration. Odunusi Emmanuel Oluwaseyifunmi is script writer, Art lover, educational consultant, prolific musician and a child of God.
