GIRL RIGHTS ARE HUMAN RIGHTS

Rights are meant to be respected and protected, not violated.

Girls in the developing world does not complete sixth grade.
70% out of the 130 million out-of-school youth are girls.

= 33,000 girls every day.

1 out of 3

What we do

Girl Child Advocacy

Our advocacy programs tackle societal problems like Gender Based Violence, Menstrual Hygiene, Illiteracy, and early marriage that affect the Girl Child negatively.

Women Empowerment

Our programs empower young women with vocational and digital skills to help them become productive members with equal opportunities in the society.

Youth Development

Our youth development programs equip young people with soft skills needed for personal development. We also provide career counseling, mentoring and coaching for young people when the need arises.
